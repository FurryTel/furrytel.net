About FurryTel

FurryTel is a non-profit organization in Wasington state. We are supported by generous donations of time and equipment by the Telecommunications History Group, Accelerate Networks, and financial donations by users like you.

We support one time donations through PayPal, as well as reoccouring subscriptions through Patreon.
