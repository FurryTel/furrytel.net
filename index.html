What is FurryTel?

FurryTel is a non profit organization in Washington state that provides networking services as a hobby. We provide equipment and experienced volunteers to provide a unique experience with networking hardware for furry related events.

Why is FurryTel

We thought it would be fun to organize around a shared goal.
